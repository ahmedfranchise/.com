Comprehensive Money Transfer Services

At Ahmed Communication & Franchise System, we provide efficient and reliable money transfer services. Our goal is to make transferring money as seamless as possible, offering a wide range of options and excellent customer support.

Supported Services

  • EasyPaisa: Transfer money quickly and securely using EasyPaisa's network of agents and retail locations.
  • JazzCash: Utilize JazzCash to send and receive money conveniently through mobile and retail channels.
  • NayaPay: Send funds to NayaPay accounts with ease, benefiting from their secure and user-friendly platform.
  • SadaPay: Use SadaPay to transfer money with confidence, backed by robust security features.
  • Other Providers: We also handle transactions for various other branchless banking services, ensuring comprehensive coverage.

How It Works

  • Account Management: We assist in opening and managing branchless banking accounts with various providers.
  • Money Transfers: Effortlessly send and receive money through our platform, with options for both domestic and international transfers.
  • Cash Withdrawals: Withdraw cash from your branchless banking account at our locations or through our network of partners.
  • Customer Support: Our knowledgeable staff provides expert assistance with any questions or issues you may encounter.
  • Secure Transactions: We prioritize the security of your transactions, using advanced encryption and fraud prevention measures.
