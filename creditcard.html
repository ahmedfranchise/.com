Credit Card Facilities

At Ahmed Communication & Franchise System, we provide comprehensive credit card facilities, including credit card bill payments and information on major credit cards available in Pakistan. Whether you're looking to manage your credit card payments or need details about different credit cards, we offer a range of services to cater to your financial needs.

Credit Card Payment Facility

  • Easy Bill Payment: Conveniently pay your credit card bills through our services.
  • Online Payment: Access online portals and apps for credit card bill payments.
  • Retail Outlets: Visit our partnered retail outlets to make credit card payments in person.
  • Customer Support: Dedicated support for handling payment issues and queries.

Major Credit Cards in Pakistan

  • Habib Bank Limited (HBL) Credit Cards: Offers a variety of credit cards with rewards, cashback, and travel benefits.
  • United Bank Limited (UBL) Credit Cards: Includes cards with competitive rates, discounts, and exclusive offers.
  • MCB Bank Credit Cards: Provides various card options for personal and business use with numerous benefits.
  • Standard Chartered Credit Cards: Features premium cards with global acceptance and numerous rewards.
  • Bank Alfalah Credit Cards: Offers cards with cashbacks, reward points, and travel perks.
  • Faysal Bank Credit Cards: Provides various options with flexible payment terms and rewards.
  • Dubai Islamic Bank Credit Cards: Shariah-compliant cards with attractive rewards and benefits.

Additional Information

  • Card Types: Choose from various types including standard, gold, platinum, and corporate cards.
  • Card Benefits: Enjoy benefits such as reward points, cashback offers, travel insurance, and exclusive discounts.
  • Fees and Charges: Be aware of annual fees, interest rates, and foreign transaction charges associated with credit cards.

How to Apply

  • Online Application: Apply for credit cards through bank websites or apps.
  • In-Branch Application: Visit bank branches to fill out credit card application forms.
  • Required Documents: Prepare necessary documents such as ID, proof of income, and address verification.
