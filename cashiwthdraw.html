Cash Withdrawals Made Easy

At Ahmed Communication & Franchise System, we offer a streamlined service for cash withdrawals from various accounts. Whether you need to withdraw cash from a bank account, a mobile account, or a branchless account, we make the process hassle-free and efficient.

Bank Account Withdrawals

  • ATMs: Withdraw cash from any ATM in Pakistan using your bank card.
  • Bank Branches: Visit any branch of major banks including:
    • Habib Bank Limited (HBL)
    • United Bank Limited (UBL)
    • National Bank of Pakistan (NBP)
    • MCB Bank Limited
    • Standard Chartered Bank
    • Bank Alfalah
  • Online Banking: Access your bank's online portal or app for easy cash transfers and withdrawals.

Mobile Account Withdrawals

  • Mobile Banking Apps: Use apps like:
    • EasyPaisa
    • JazzCash
    • NayaPay
    • SadaPay
    • UPaisa
  • Cash-Out Points: Withdraw cash from authorized cash-out points or agents for mobile accounts.

Branchless Account Withdrawals

  • Retailer Agents: Access cash from various branchless banking agents across Pakistan.
  • Partner Networks: Utilize our network of partner retailers to withdraw cash from branchless accounts like:
    • EasyPaisa
    • JazzCash
    • NayaPay
    • SadaPay

Additional Services

  • Instant Transfers: Immediate cash withdrawals with instant transfer options.
  • Transaction Support: Assistance with transactions and resolving any issues during cash withdrawals.
  • Customer Service: Dedicated support to handle queries related to cash withdrawals and account management.
