Mobile SIM Facilities

Ahmed Communication & Franchise System offers comprehensive mobile SIM facilities to meet all your telecommunication needs. Our services include the issuance of new SIMs, SIM duplication, number portability, and more. We work with all major mobile network operators in Pakistan to ensure seamless connectivity and reliable service for our customers.

Services Offered

  • New SIM Issuance: Get a new SIM card for your mobile phone from any network provider.
  • SIM Duplication: Duplicate your existing SIM card in case of loss or damage.
  • Number Portability: Transfer your number from one network provider to another effortlessly.
  • SIM Activation: Activate your SIM card quickly and efficiently.
  • Customer Support: Assistance with all SIM-related issues and queries.

Major Mobile Network Operators in Pakistan

  • Jazz: Provides a wide range of prepaid and postpaid plans, including data, talk time, and SMS packages.
  • Ufone: Offers various SIM options with competitive pricing and extensive coverage.
  • Telenor: Features a diverse portfolio of SIM cards with flexible plans and offers.
  • Zong: Known for its affordable SIM packages and strong network presence.
  • Warid: Provides SIM cards with unique features and plans tailored to customer needs.

Additional Services

  • SIM Replacement: Replace your old or damaged SIM card with a new one.
  • Prepaid and Postpaid Plans: Choose from various prepaid and postpaid plans based on your usage.
  • International Roaming: Activate international roaming services for seamless communication abroad.

How to Apply

  • Visit Our Shop: Come to our retail outlet to apply for new SIMs or request other services.
  • Required Documents: Provide necessary identification documents such as CNIC for SIM issuance.
  • Online Application: Some services may be available online through the network provider's website or app.
